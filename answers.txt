What are some Pros and Cons of Agile development (vs non-Agile)?

Pros:
- Constant added value to the customer (no delayed testing/deployment)
- Consistent collaboration with stakeholders and requirements update through iterative approach

Cons:
- Fragmented output as the deliverables are delivered in parts
- Greater time commitment

Explain the role and responsibilities of the Scrum Master vs the Product Owner.

Scrum Master manages all scrum-related tasks (JIRA, sprintsand meetings, standups, etc.).
On the other hand, the Product Owner sets direction for the team, handles release and stakeholder management.

What are the 3 types of meetings that happen during a sprint cycle?

1. Sprint Planning Meeting:
- Determine what to complete in the coming sprint and start it
- Determine story points and assign stories to team members
2. Daily Scrum/Standup Meeting:
- Team members discuss the work completed
- Discuss the plan for the day and current blockers
3. Sprint Retrospective Meeting:
- Discuss completed work and move the incomplete work to backlog
- Close sprint
